Southern California

Southern California is one of the largest warehousing and logistics regions in the US. Fed by the Ports of Los Angeles and Long Beach, 40% of the nation's imported goods travel through the freeways, freight rail, airports, and over 1.3 billion square feet of warehouses in the region.
